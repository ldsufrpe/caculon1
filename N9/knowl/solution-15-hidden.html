derivando implicitamente em relação a \(x\) fornece

\begin{equation*} 20 x^{4} + 14 x^{} y + 7 x^{2} \, \frac{dy}{dx} - 2 y^{5} - 10 x y^{4} \, \frac{dy}{dx} = 0, \end{equation*}

ou

\begin{equation*} (7x^{2}-10xy^{4}) \, \frac{dy}{dx} = -(20x^{4}+14xy-2y^{5}). \end{equation*}

Logo,

\begin{equation*} \frac{dy}{dx} = -\frac{20x^{4}+14xy-2y^{5}}{7x^{2}-10xy^{4}}. \end{equation*}
