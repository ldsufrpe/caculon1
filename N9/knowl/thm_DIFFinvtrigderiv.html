Teorema 1.11.

As derivadas das funções trigonométricas inversas são

\begin{align*} \dfrac{\dd}{\dd x} \arcsin(x) \amp= \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \amp \dfrac{\dd}{\dd x} \arccsc(x) \amp= -\frac{1}{|x|\sqrt{x^2-1}}\\ \dfrac{\dd}{\dd x} \arccos(x) \amp= -\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \amp \dfrac{\dd}{\dd x} \arcsec(x) \amp= \frac{1}{|x|\sqrt{x^2-1}}\\ \dfrac{\dd}{\dd x} \arctan(x) \amp= \frac{1}{1+x^2} \amp \dfrac{\dd}{\dd x} \arccot(x) \amp= -\frac{1}{1+x^2} \end{align*}
