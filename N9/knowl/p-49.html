Parágrafo

Agora \(\frac{11\pi}{16}=\frac{\pi}{2} +\frac{3\pi}{16}\) então

\begin{equation*} \sin\Big(\frac{11\pi}{16}\Big) =\sin\Big(\frac{\pi}{2}+\frac{3\pi}{16}\Big) =\sin\Big(\frac{\pi}{2}-\frac{3\pi}{16}\Big) =\sin\Big(\frac{5\pi}{16}\Big) \end{equation*}

e, visto que \(\frac{5\pi}{16}\) está entre \(-\frac{\pi}{2}\) e \(\frac{\pi}{2}\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} \arcsin\Big(\sin\Big(\frac{11\pi}{16}\Big)\Big) =\frac{5\pi}{16}\qquad\Big(\text{e não $\frac{11\pi}{16}$}\Big). \end{equation*}
