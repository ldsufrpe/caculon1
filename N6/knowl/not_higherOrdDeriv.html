Definição 1.28.

  • \(f''(x)\) e \(f^{(2)}(x)\) e \(\dfrac{\dd^2 f}{\dd x^2}( x)\) todos significam \(\dfrac{\dd }{\dd x}\big(\dfrac{\dd }{\dd x}f(x)\big)\)

  • \(f'''(x)\) e \(f^{(3)}(x)\) e \(\dfrac{\dd^3 f}{\dd x^3}(x)\) todos significam \(\dfrac{\dd }{\dd x}\big(\dfrac{\dd }{\dd x}\big(\dfrac{\dd }{\dd x}f(x)\big)\big)\)

  • \(f^{(4)}(x)\) e \(\dfrac{\dd^4 f}{\dd x^4}(x)\) ambos significam \(\dfrac{\dd }{\dd x}\big(\dfrac{\dd }{\dd x}\big(\dfrac{\dd }{\dd x}\big(\dfrac{\dd }{\dd x}f(x)\big)\big) \big)\)
