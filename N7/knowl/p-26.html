\begin{align*} f(u) \amp= \cos(u) \amp f'(u) \amp= -\sin(u)\\ g(x) \amp= 3x-2 \amp g'(x) \amp= 3 \end{align*}

Então a A Regra da Cadeia — version 1 fornece:

\begin{align*} F'(x) \amp= f'\big(g(x)\big) \cdot g'(x)\\ \amp= -\sin\big( g(x) \big) \cdot 3\\ \amp= -3 \sin(3x-2) \end{align*}
