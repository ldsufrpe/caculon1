Definição 1.10.

Let \(a\in\bbbr\) and let \(f(x)\) be defined on an open interval that contains \(a\text{.}\) The derivative of \(f(x)\) at \(x=a\) is denoted \(f'(a)\) and is defined by

\begin{gather*} f'(a)=\lim_{h\rightarrow 0}\frac{f\big(a+h\big)-f(a)}{h} \end{gather*}

if the limit exists.
