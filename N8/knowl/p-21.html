Parágrafo

We can extend Theorem Teorema 1.1 to compute the derivative of logarithms of other bases in a straightforward way. Since for any positive \(a \neq 1\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} \log_a x &= \frac{\log x}{\log a} = \frac{1}{\log a} \cdot \log x & \text{since $a$ is a constant}\\ \dfrac{\dd}{\dd x} \log_a x &= \frac{1}{\log a} \cdot \frac{1}{x} \end{align*}
