Solução 1.4.5.1.

Primeiro reescreva as funções dadas usando as regras dos logaritmos.

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} f(x) \amp =\amp \ln (6 x^5) \\ \amp \amp \\ \amp =\amp \ln (6) + 5 \ln x \\ \amp \amp \\ f'(x) \amp =\amp \displaystyle 0 + 5 \cdot \frac{1}{x} = \frac{5}{x}. \end{array} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} g(x) \amp =\amp \ln (\sqrt[6] x) \\ \amp \amp \\ \amp =\amp \displaystyle \frac{1}{6} \ln x \\ \amp \amp \\ g'(x) \amp =\amp \displaystyle \frac{1}{6} \cdot \frac{1}{x} = \frac{1}{6 x}. \end{array} \end{equation*}
