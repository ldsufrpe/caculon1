Solução 1.4.4.1.

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \displaystyle\frac{d}{dx}\left[ \log_a x \right] \amp =\amp \displaystyle \frac{d}{dx}\left[ \frac{\ln x}{\ln a} \right] \\ \amp \amp \\ \amp =\amp \displaystyle\frac{1}{\ln a} \cdot \frac{d}{dx}\left[ \ln x \right] \\ \amp \amp \\ \amp =\amp \displaystyle\frac{1}{\ln a}\cdot \frac{1}{x} \\ \amp \amp \\ \amp =\amp \displaystyle\frac{1}{x \ln a} \end{array} \end{equation*}

Usando o resultado acima com \(a = 5\text{,}\)

\begin{equation*} \displaystyle\frac{d}{dx}\left[ \log_{5} x \right] = \displaystyle\frac{1}{x \ln 5}. \end{equation*}
