Item.
Some trigonometry gives us
\begin{align*} \tan \varphi \amp= \frac{p}{x}\\ \tan (\varphi+\theta) \amp= \frac{p+h}{x} \end{align*}
Thus
\begin{align*} \varphi \amp= \arctan \frac{p}{x}\\ \varphi+\theta \amp= \arctan \frac{p+h}{x} \end{align*}
and so
\begin{align*} \theta \amp= \arctan \frac{p+h}{x} - \arctan\frac{p}{x}. \end{align*}
