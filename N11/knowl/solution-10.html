Solução 1.6.8.1.

Usaremos a identidade \((\sec x)^{\csc x}=e^{\ln\left((\sec x)^{\csc x}\right)}\text{,}\) e, portanto, deve calcular

\begin{equation*} \eqalign{ \lim_{x\to0} \ln\left((\sec x)^{\csc x}\right) \amp =\lim_{x\to0} \csc x\left(\ln(\sec x)\right) =\lim_{x\to0} {\ln(\sec x) \over \sin x}\cr \amp =\lim_{x\to0} {\left(1\over\sec x\right)(\sec x\tan x) \over \cos x} =\lim_{x\to0} {\tan x\over \cos x} =0.\cr } \end{equation*}

Portanto, \(\displaystyle \lim_{x\to0} (\sec x)^{\csc x} = \lim_{x\to0+} e^{\ln\left((\sec x)^{\csc x}\right)} = e^0 = 1\text{.}\)
