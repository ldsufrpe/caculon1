Definição 1.2.

xml:id="def_1_6_2"

A function \(f(x)\) is continuous from the right at \(a\) if

\begin{align*} \lim_{x\to a^+} f(x) \amp= f(a). \end{align*}

Similarly a function \(f(x)\) is continuous from the left at \(a\) if

\begin{align*} \lim_{x\to a^-} f(x) \amp= f(a) \end{align*}
